About Erica

I never knew what I wanted to be when I grew up. I'm still figuring that out. Recently I quit a good job to begin a master's in technical communication and information design at Illinois Institute of Technology. I have an undergraduate degree in marketing and a training background. While in school, I'm working as a research assistant and freelance writer, editor, and designer, and I have a small consignment business on eBay.

That's a little bit about me. Feel free to take a look at my resume and portfolio to learn more, and let me know if you have a project that could use my help.
